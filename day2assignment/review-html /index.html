How To Make a Sandwich

  • Purchase necessary sandwich ingredients of your choosing. In this case, we'll choose peanut butter, jelly, sandwich bread, and a butter knife
  • Bring ingredients home. Put on counter, preferably on a cutting board.
  • Open sandwich bag with the opening at the top and take out 2 slices of bread.
